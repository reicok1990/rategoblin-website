How It Works

From uploaded quote to actionable insight in four simple steps.

1
📄

Upload

Upload contractor quotes in PDF or Excel format. Rate Goblin accepts the document formats you already use.

2
🧠

Extract

AI reads and extracts individual line items, unit rates, quantities, and descriptions from each quote automatically.

3
📊

Benchmark

Extracted rates are compared against your historical project data and market benchmarks to highlight anomalies.

4
✅

Decide

Identify potential savings, flag overpriced items, and build an evidence-based position for commercial negotiations.

Features Overview

Everything a quantity surveyor needs to streamline procurement intelligence.

📥

AI Quote Extraction

Automatically extract line-item rates, quantities, and descriptions from contractor quotes in PDF and Excel formats. Reduce hours of manual data entry to minutes.

📈

Rate Benchmarking

Compare extracted rates against your historical project data to spot outliers and pricing trends. Build confidence in your commercial recommendations.

🏷

NRM Classification

AI-assisted mapping of line items to NRM1 and NRM2 cost categories for consistent reporting. Maintain structured cost data aligned with industry standards.

💡

Value Engineering

Identify opportunities for cost optimisation by analysing rate variations across trades and suppliers. Support data-driven value engineering conversations.

📋

Cost Plan Management

Organise and manage cost plans with structured data from extracted quotes. Track budget performance and forecast project costs with greater accuracy.

🛡

RICS Compliance

Built with RICS professional standards and guidance in mind. Audit trails and structured outputs support transparent, defensible procurement decisions.

🌍

Community Rate Database

Opt in to contribute anonymised rate data to a growing industry benchmarking pool. Help build the UK's most comprehensive construction rate intelligence — and benefit from stronger, data-driven benchmarks powered by real project rates.
